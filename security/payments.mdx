## Payment information

Budgiter leverages the tools and security of our payment processor to handle payment information. Budgiter builds on a platform designed to protect your data with AES encryption and isolated infrastructure that doesn’t share any credentials with Stripe’s primary services. More information on online payments can be found on [Stripe's website](https://stripe.com/guides/introduction-to-online-payments).
